Product Design

Vault App

A personal finance and savings app designed around building healthy money habits — with a calm, trustworthy visual language that makes finance feel approachable.

Role
Product Designer
Year
2022
Tools
Figma

Overview

The Challenge

Finance apps often suffer from one of two problems: they're overwhelming (too much data, too many features) or underwhelming (no insight, no motivation). Vault aimed to sit in the middle — simple enough for everyday use, smart enough to actually change behaviour.

The design challenge was to make saving money feel rewarding rather than restrictive, using gentle nudges, clear progress visualisation, and a visual tone that feels warm and trustworthy rather than cold and corporate.

Process

Design Process

01

Research

Reviewed existing fintech apps (Monzo, Revolut, Plum) and conducted user interviews about savings habits.

02

Strategy

Defined core features: savings pots, spending insights, goal tracking, and weekly summaries.

03

Visual Language

Developed a warm, earthy palette with soft typography to reduce financial anxiety.

04

UI Design

Polished high-fidelity screens with a focus on data visualisation and empty state design.

Screens

App Screens

The app centres around a clean dashboard that surfaces the most important number (your savings rate), with drill-down views for individual pots and spending categories. Every screen aims to answer one clear question, not five.
